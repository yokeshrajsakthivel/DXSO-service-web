Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can DXSO deploy? +

Our agent-based deployment is rapid. For most SMBs and Mid-market organizations, full rollout can be completed within 24-48 hours with minimal disruption.

Do you support hybrid cloud environments? +

Yes, DXSO XDR is designed for hybrid environments, covering on-premise endpoints, private clouds, and public cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP).

Is the SOC 24/7? +

Our automated monitoring is 24/7. Human-led SOC analysis and distinct response times depend on your tier (Elite vs. Premium), with Premium offering full 24/7 SOC coverage.
